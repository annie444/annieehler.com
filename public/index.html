Projects

Cystic Fibrosis Foundation

Geneticist studying gene regulation in lungs affected by Cystic Fibrosis, Bronchtis, and COPD. Included intense statistical conmputation and rudementary machine learning.

Adverse Childhood Experiences

Hosted an event to educate the public about rough home experiences and mental health in today’s youth. 200 tickets were purchased in the first week sales opened.

Vitis Herbarium

Researcher at University of Northern Iowa’s botanical labs, researching the metamorphic distinguishing factors in the species of the vitis phylum through herbarium, ampelography, and metamorphic image analysis.

Humans of Cedar Rapids

Began a blog group in Cedar Rapids, IA to help voice the stories of Iowa’s unsung heroes, and provide a platform to begin the discussions of our issues that people would usually shy away from.

Physics for Creativity

Reimagined high school advanced physics curriculum for the modern world in an effort to spark more interest in STEM fields, especially in minority populations.
